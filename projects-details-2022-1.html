3-D TOPMAP

  • Category: Method Presentation and Implementation
  • Client: Philippine Trigon Shipyard
  • Project date: January 3, 2021

Project Details:

LASER BASED 3-D TOPOGRAPHICAL MAPPING OF VESSEL OUTER SHELL (3-D TOPMAP) is the application of laser measuring device to generate the lines of a ship hull.

The lines can be used to define the geometry of the hull in a 3d software and later be used for ship calculations.
