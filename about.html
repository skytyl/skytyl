Ship Design and Consultancy

SkyTyl Ship Design and Consultancy specializes in the formulation of suitable engineering solutions that allow the integration and harmonization of the requirements by the shipowners to that of the capability of the Philippine local shipyards.

This makes the implementation of vessel new construction, modification or extensive ship repairs efficient and cost-effective.

The extensive use of updated ship design software and hardware systems, strong collaboration with Maritime Industry Authority (MARINA) and continuous coordination with the local shipyards has allowed SkyTyl to generate valuable experiences in the generation of practical and implementable ship designs and engineering solutions since its inception in 2002.

Marine structures are one of the most complex engineering systems which involve not only the optimization and efficient use of limited spaces, but as well as to ensure the safety of the transport of goods and people. The practice of multi-disciplinary integrated ship design is still in its infancy stage here in the Philippines. This is primarily due to the limited exposure of current domestic technologies in the complimentary use and application of several computer software systems.

SkyTyl's main tools in the process of obtaining the most practical design as well as suitability to the requirements of the domestic shipping industry is extensive and continuing research in the applications of international rules and standards in the construction of ship structures incorporated to within the limits of the capacity and capability of the local shipyard industry. This requires the comprehensive and continuous technical cooperation with the shipyards, MARINA Administration, technology and machinery suppliers

This is the forte of Skytyl Ship Design and Consultancy. SkyTyl started and continues to develop these integrated systems since 2002. The projects involved construction, modification and even the prescription of suitable engineering procedures in the management of the production processes. The experiences accumulated thru the years of practice in the domestic shipyard and shipping industries has allowed SkyTyl to be a very reliant and responsible partner to both the ship owners, shipyards and MARINA in the production of engineering solutions without the necessity of incurring high costs or unsafe marine structures.
